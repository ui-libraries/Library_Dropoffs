Off-Campus Book Drop-Off Locations


Digital Scholarship & Publishing Studio, University of Iowa Libraries

If you are away from campus and have books from your university library, you may be wondering how to return them. This map shows the many locations where you can return your books. University of Iowa students may drop off their books at any library listed on the map. Students of other schools throughout Iowa may use all of the Iowa libraries on this map, except for the two public libraries. Use the filter button in the top left corner to find out where you can return books if you are not a University of Iowa student. Click on the map icons for library contact information.
